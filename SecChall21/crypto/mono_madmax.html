Solution

Just reading the title of the challenge, it was clear we are facing a monoalphabetic substitution cipher. A quick google search led me to this site. Pasting the text and decoding it, gives us readable information: the script of Mad Max 2: The Road Warrior. One “feature”, this site has, is it only gives a fixed number of characters as output, so I had to move the flag from the middle of the file to the top. (It was easy to find, thanks to the curly brackets)

The acquired flag is:

cd21{HIDDEN}
